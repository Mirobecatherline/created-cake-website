About Us

This company was founded in the year 2016 under the leadership of Catherine Mirobe. It has branches in Kisii and Juja.It has maintained to be one of the best pastry shop in kenya . The bakery has maintained its good customer service by offering home delivery services of high end quality cakes.

The bakery is opening its braches all over the contry to reach all customers .Its share holders are spread throught the wold. The company offers both bakery services and catering services in all kids of events.
